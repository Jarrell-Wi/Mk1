population - whole set of items of interest
Cencus - observes/measures every member of a population
Sample - observations from a subset used to learn about a whole population
sampling units - individuals of  a population
sampling frame - When sampling units are given names/numbers
systematic sampling - using intervals to select sampling units
stratified sampling - dividing population into groups and selecting Samples from each
